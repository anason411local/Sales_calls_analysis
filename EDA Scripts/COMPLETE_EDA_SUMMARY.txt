   - Extended calls: >5 minutes (49.3%)
   - Nearly 50/50 split at 5-minute mark

2. DOMINANT CATEGORY:
   - >5 minutes is the LARGEST single category (49.3%)
   - 602 calls exceed 5 minutes
   - Indicates strong customer engagement

3. SHORT CALLS:
   - 33.4% of calls are under 3 minutes
   - 8.3% are under 1 minute (potential issues)
   - Gradual decline from 1-5 minutes

4. DISTRIBUTION PATTERN:
   - NOT a normal/Gaussian distribution
   - Right-skewed with heavy tail
   - Typical for call duration data
   - Mean > Median (483s > 293s)

5. OUTLIERS:
   - 7.9% of calls are unusually long
   - May represent complex interactions
   - Opportunity for quality review
